SHUTR LAB

Canon tether.
Built for macOS.

Native USB tethering for Canon EOS cameras. No Electron, no EOS Utility — just a small Mac app that catches every shot, files it correctly, and stays out of your way.

Install

  1. Open the downloaded .dmg
  2. Drag SHUTR LAB to your Applications folder
  3. Launch it — connect your Canon over USB and you're tethered

What it does

  • Real USB tether to Canon EOS bodies (R6 Mark II tested)
  • Auto-downloads every capture into your shoot folders
  • Recital workflow: stations, solos/duos/trios, groups
  • Full exposure controls: Tv, Av, ISO, EV, WB, picture style
  • Card import, intervalometer, bulb timer, batch rename
  • Updates itself — no manual re-downloads

Requirements

macOS 13.0 or later
Apple Silicon or Intel
USB connection to a supported Canon EOS body
